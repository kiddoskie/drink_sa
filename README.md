Drinks SA 🍹
The first truly South African drinks education ecosystem.

Drinks SA is a web-based educational platform designed to bridge the gap between traditional South African beverage culture and modern, sustainable industry standards. From the unique "terroir" of the Cape Floral Kingdom to the science of traditional brewing, Drinks SA provides industry professionals and enthusiasts with the knowledge to excel.

🚀 Features
Micro-Learning Modules: Bite-sized lessons covering everything from whisky maturation to gin botanicals.

Interactive Quizzes: Test your knowledge with module-specific quizzes designed to reinforce learning.

Taste Discovery: A personalized pathway to understand and identify unique flavor profiles.

SA Passport: Track your educational journey and milestones through the South African beverage landscape.

Industry Portal: Insights and tools specifically curated for brands and service professionals.

🛠️ Built With
React.js - The core framework for the user interface.

Vite - High-performance build tool and dev server.

Modern CSS/JS - Clean, modular design with responsive layouts.
